slug: "frontmatter"
order: 2
---

- Metadata for your Markdown
- Variables that can later be injected into your components

- Must be:
  - At the top of the file
  - Valid YAML
  - Between triple dashed lines

### Frontmatter example

```markdown
---
title: My Frontmatter Title
example_number: 1
example_boolean: true  
---
```
